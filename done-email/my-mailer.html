Managing Retention Campaigns

You are probably already familiar with the TechFinancials back office marketing management system that enables you to build and track campaigns with affiliates and networks, in order to attract new users.

You might be less familiar with the system’s ability to create and manage retention campaigns. More and more TechFinancials partners are using tools to measure what is happening with existing users, in order to identify inactive users or those at risk of

becoming inactive – such as those with low balances, for example, who will not be able to continue trading unless they make a new deposit. These customers are excellent targets for a retention campaign, possibly offering a new bonus or other benefit for making a deposit and engaging in trading activities.

As opposed to recruitment campaigns, retention campaigns are aimed at users you have direct access to, and so they can be offered by email, or through notifications on the trading site.

What are the steps?

Follow these steps in the TechFinancials back office to run a retention campaign:

1.Define a marketer. This is a “fake” marketer, just used for the campaign.
2.Create a new campaign. In this step you assign the campaign to the marketer you created, and add bonuses.
3.Create a retention (OFRTC) tracking code. This is how the user gets their bonus and enters the wagering cycle.
4.Search/select the customers to send the campaign to.
